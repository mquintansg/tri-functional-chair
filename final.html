More About Us

We are a small design studio focused on creating furniture for modern living. Our work is driven by the idea that everyday objects should be both functional and adaptable to changing lifestyles. As living spaces become smaller and more flexible, furniture needs to evolve with them.

Our team combines product design, digital tools, and creative experimentation to develop pieces that simplify daily life. We believe that good design should be intuitive, durable, and visually clean. Rather than adding complexity, we aim to reduce it, creating objects that feel natural to use.

The Flip Lounge was developed from this philosophy. It explores how a single piece of furniture can serve multiple purposes through a simple movement. By rotating the structure, the chair shifts from an upright seat to a relaxed lounge position, offering different ways to sit within the same compact form.

Our design process blends traditional design thinking with new technologies. We experiment with digital modeling and AI-assisted tools to explore shapes, ergonomics, and functionality, allowing us to test many ideas before arriving at a refined solution.

At the core of our work is a commitment to simplicity, versatility, and comfort. We design furniture that adapts to people’s lives — not the other way around.

Flip Lounge

At Q Estudios we believe that style matters in every type of space. For this reason, we want to bring our brand into multifunctional designs that combine aesthetics, comfort, and practicality.

The Flip Lounge is an innovative chair that adapts to your needs. Its rotating structure allows you to easily switch from an upright position, perfect for working or reading, to a reclined position ideal for relaxing.

With clean lines and an intuitive design, this chair combines functionality and elegance in a single piece of furniture, making any small space versatile and comfortable.

Included in the purchase:

  • The chair fully assembled and ready to use.
  • Two headrest extensions that can also be used as a footrest depending on your preference.
